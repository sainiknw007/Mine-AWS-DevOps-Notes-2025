ex8:
---
write a python program to print '*'s in floyd's diamond pattern?

sample input:

	num=5

expected output:

    *
   * *
  * * *
 * * * *
* * * * *
 * * * *
  * * * 
   * *
    *

	demo.py
	-------
num=int(input("enter your number: "))
for r in range(1,num+1):
    for c in range(1,num-r+1):
        print(" ",end="")
    for c in range(1,r+1):
        print("*",end=" ")
    print()
for r in range(1,num):
    for c in range(1,r+1):
        print(" ",end="")
    for c in range(1,num-r+1):
        print("*",end=" ")
    print()
